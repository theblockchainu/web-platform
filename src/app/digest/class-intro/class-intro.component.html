Host a Class on Peerbuds Earn money giving people the best online learning experience. What are Peerbuds Classes? Peerbuds Classes are interactive online classes designed and led by peers from our global community. While not everyone can attend our in-person learning experiences, they should have access to the same experience online. Classes go beyond creating a typical video course by allowing you to provide interactive live sessions for the cohort your teaching. They also consist of projects, videos, assignments, and a place for your cohort to discuss and stay connected on the Peerbuds Platform.
