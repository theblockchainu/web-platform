Learning is continuous, not episodic

Education is no longer episodic that could be gained from 15 years of school. Most people now learn their entire lives even from individuals or informal groups like a community center or an app.

From degrees to reputation metrics

Degrees do not do justice to the modern day learner. 'Gyan' is an objective measure of your knowledge that gets continuously recorded on a global distributed ledger.

Teach for free, earn back Karma as rewards

'Gyan' is your stake in the economy. 'Karma' is your earning through weekly rewards or payments. All your earning from every Gyan is tracked to give feedback on your teachers.
