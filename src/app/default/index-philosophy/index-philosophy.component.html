What problem are we solving

Our designed to revamp an already evolving educational ecosystem. As the primary educational paradigm shifts to one of co-learning, PeerBuds wants to ensure that alongside this co-learning, all our users are also being awarded for the learning that takes place. One of the key deficiencies in traditional educational models is the discrete learning that takes place through K-12 and post-secondary, with that being the conclusion of learning for the majority of the population. However, in a knowledge economy, it is necessary for members of society to experience continuous learning.

The current generation of students embody this concept, they are working learners who seek diverse learning and working experiences through various stages of their lives.The future of learning is already evolving today, as we see in the evolving content and methods of learning. This re-definition of learning is leading to the creation of new pathways to success, positive changes in life satisfaction and ultimately, a revamped economy.
