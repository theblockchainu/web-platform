What are Blockchain University sessions?

The Blockchain University Sessions are interactive online sessions designed and led by peers from our global community. While not everyone can attend our in-person learning sessions, they should have access to the same session online. Sessions go beyond creating a typical video course by allowing you to provide interactive live sessions for the cohort your teaching. They also consist of projects, videos, assignments, and a place for your cohort to discuss and stay connected on the The Blockchain University Platform.
