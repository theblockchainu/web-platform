Getting Started
  1. Sign Up

    Start by registering on the platform. At the Blockchain University, user accounts are of a singular type - allowing everyone to both learn and teach at the same time. Go ahead and register yourself, and when you want to begin teaching.

  2. Set Up Your Teaching Profile

    If this is your first time teaching on the platform, you’ll need to set up your teaching profile by selecting your preferred mode of teaching experiences - from online courses, in-person workshops, mentor sessions and more.

    Our portal will take you through the process as you complete the setup.

  3. Upload Your Coursework

    Depending on your choice of teaching model, you’ll need to upload your course material - from videos for online courses, to accompanying information for in-person workshops, etc

  4. Start Learning and Earning

    That’s it. Now start off with your teaching experiences, give the gift of a reinvented education to your learners, and get rewarded for your time and knowledge using one0x.
